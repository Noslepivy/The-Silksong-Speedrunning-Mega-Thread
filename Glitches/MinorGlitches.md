# Minor Glitches

A collection of glitches deemed as minor, and allowed for NMG runs, by the SRC rules.

> [!WARNING]  
> You should always double check the [SRC rules here](https://github.com/hk-speedrunning/Silksong-Rules/blob/main/README.md) before submitting a run. Take this list of glitches simply as a resource of explanations, as it may become outdated compared to what is or isn't allowed.
