## Tool Pogos
- [Voltvessel Pogos](#voltvessel-pogos)

>### Voltvessel Pogos
>Patched as of 1.0.29242.  
>On patches 1.0.28891 or lower, you can use either [Snare Setter](https://hollowknight.wiki/w/Snare_Setter) or [Delver's Drill](https://hollowknight.wiki/w/Delver%27s_Drill) to hit, and gain height from, thrown [Voltvessels](https://hollowknight.wiki/w/Voltvessels) in their Bola form.  
>The inputs will vary per skip, so you may want to check out a [categories specific skips](/README.md#skips) but the general idea remains of -  
>If grounded:  
> Throw Vessels > Hit the vessels if you'd like them to go in a certain direction (usually either with nail or sawtooth) > "Pogo" with Snare setter or Delvers drill > Which can combo into ...  
>When in-air:  
> Double Jump > Throw vessels at a wall (or if you have sawtooth equipped, you can use sawtooth to split them) >Float for a moment > "Pogo" the bola with snare setter or drill > ... Repeat
